How should quick jumps work?

You want a unified stream but fast access to each section. Here are 3 approaches.

Sticky pill bar (Recommended)

Tiny pill icons pinned at the top. Click to scroll to section. Highlights which section you're currently viewing. Almost invisible — just 20px tall.
